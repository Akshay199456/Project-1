Instructions

In this game, you will have the opportunity to select 1 pokemon from a collection of pokemon to challenge the computer's 1 pokemon. Each pokemon has a name and type associated with it. Every pokemon type is weak against as well as strong against other pokemon types.

Fire is weak against water and strong against grass. Water is weak against grass and strong against fire. Grass is weak against fire and strong against water. Use this knowledge wisely while battling the computer's pokemon! Defeat the computer's pokemon to win the battle.

Now go out there. A journey awaits!
